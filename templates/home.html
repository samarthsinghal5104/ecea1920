What is ECEA

The ECEA is a student body consisting of B.Tech. and M.Tech. students of ECE branch, guided by a faculty advisor. Our main aim is to provide quality content for the college, especially for the students of Electronics and Communication Engineering Department. We conduct various events and workshops, both technical and non-technical. We are always ready to learn new things and stay updated with latest technology in order to make people of the department and also of the entire college aware of them.

VISION OF THE DEPARTMENT:

Through its faculty, Department of ECE is a driving force.

  • Create an educational and friendly environment to prepare students to meet the challenges of modern Electronics and Communication Engineering industry from the experiences of faculties and seniors in technical knowledge and innovative approaches. Also to build unity and the sense of teamwork among its members.

MISSION OF THE DEPARTMENT:

  • To create learning, development and testing environment to meet ever challenging needs of the Electronics Industry.
  • To create Leadership qualities and Industry interaction for student benefits.
  • Allowing Students to share reservoir of experience in education and co-curricular activities and to work in dynamic teams.
  • To invoke the social responsibility of each individual through social involvement activities.
